About Me Page

Hello, my name is Anthony Islas. I am currently in my second semester at Northwest Vista College in San Antonio Texas. My current major is digital media and cinema productions, and I hope to one day become a film writer. I have always loved storytelling since I was little, and have had so many stories flowing through my head that I want to tell the world. I feel like film is the best way to tell these stories, and I would hope to not only write my own stories one day, but help adapt other peoples stories and put it on the big screen.

As for my hobbies I of course love to read books and watch films and TV shows. Books and movies are the reason I love storytelling in the first place so I continue to keep up with the latest in films and books. My other main hobbies are video games and Youtube. I have been playing video games for as long as I can remember, and I continue to play them to this day because it's a good way for me to relieve stress after a long day and gives me time to relax. As for Youtube, I like to watch video essay over movies and books, because I enjoy hearing other people's perspective on movies and books I have watched/read.
